Our Menu

We love to cook.

What started out as helping out at the school cafeteria and shop, organically grew into a catering business.



Inspired by the simplicity and fresh ingredients of Jamie Oliver's tasty but simple meals, an insatiable desire to read any recipe book we can find, and trying out meals at restaurants, Esta and Tracey are the two cooks that is the heart and soul of 2 Can Do Catering.


No challenge is too big for this team and whether you want an extravagant wedding reception menu or a romantic picnic basked or just a delicious home-cooked meal without needing to leave your home, we've got your covered.
